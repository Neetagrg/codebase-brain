Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! The Starter plan is free forever. Professional and Enterprise plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

How does billing work?

Professional plans are billed per user per month. You can add or remove users at any time, and we'll prorate the charges. Annual plans save 20%.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your knowledge bases at any time. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.

Is my code secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Enterprise plans include additional security features like SSO and audit logs.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@codebasebrain.ai for details.
